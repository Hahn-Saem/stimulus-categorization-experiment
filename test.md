# Stimulus Categorization Experiment - Manual Test Description
### What to Expect: (as of Project Check-in #1):
The user should expect to see poorly formatted instructions appearing on the screen after opening the experiment in the terminal. 

As of 4/3/26, the instructions are poorly formatted, with some of the words appearing beyond the size of the screen. From this point onward, the participant will be in the driver's seat, controlling the pace of the remainder of the experiment. 

Per the instructions, the participant should be able to click the SPACE bar to begin the experiment. The screen should now display an image of a dog on the screen. By clicking the 'f' or 'j' key, the participant will be able to proceed with the remaining part of the experiment. After the image of the dog, the participant will see a picture of a shoe.

Finally, a message notifying the participant that they have completed the experiment should appear on the screen. 

To exit out of the program, the user will click the SPACE bar.
